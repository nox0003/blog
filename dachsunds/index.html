When we think of a Dachshund dog, we imagine a short little hotdog with a name hard to pronounce. So we call them with their popular nicknames like Wiener-Dog, Sausage Dog, Doxie, and more. While their name might be confusing, their innocent look melts our hearts each time we see them. We know you are pretty excited to see their adorable pictures. But before that, we want to tell you something about them. Originally, dachshunds have bred to hunt badgers and other tunneling animals. Fast forward to 2021, these cuties now make adorable additions to the family. Scroll down and let these furry babies steal your heart.
